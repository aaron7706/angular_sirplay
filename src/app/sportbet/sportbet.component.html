B2C sports betting software

B2C (Business-to-Consumer) sports betting software refers to the technology and platform used by sportsbook operators to offer sports betting services directly to end-users .

This type of software enables consumers to place bets on various sports events through web or mobile applications. Here are some key features and components typically found in B2C sports betting software:

  • User Registration and Account Management: Allows users to create accounts, manage personal information, and track betting history.
  • Payment Integration: Supports various payment methods for depositing and withdrawing funds securely.
  • Customer Support: Provides customer support options, such as live chat, email, and phone support, to assist users with inquiries and issues.
  • Affiliate Programs: Offers affiliate programs for marketing and promotion, allowing partners to earn commissions for referring users.
