Sportsbook iFrame

Sportsbook iFrame allow you to enable the sportsbook module in a few days through the integration of API requests. Furthermore, the Sportsbook service is completely managed by us and we guarantee the widest programming of online events and markets.

Types of sportsbook iframe

The Sportsbook iFrame solutions are three and based on the channel used, we provide the most suitable for you:

  • iFrame for Desktop , for computers of any resolution with different and customizable layouts.
  • iFrame for Terminals and kiosks SSBT , the layout is studied to adapt to touchscreen systems and the user experience is minimal to allow inexperienced players to place bets.

Furthermore through API it is possible to customize graphics and game functions



Fully managed sportsbook

Our Sportsbook iFrame includes the provision of the odds data feed and configuration of the main betting settings. The software embeds many features and tools to manage the odds margins and configurations autonomously. But if you want to focus on the Players management and marketing only, we provide full managed trading services and support.
